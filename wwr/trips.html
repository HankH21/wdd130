Trips

Family Float

This is an entry-level plan designed for beginners and families. There are only a few rapids to let people have an introductive experience of easy whitewater rafting. At the same time, people can enjoy and relax the beauty of Hualien River.

Before this adventure you will take a 30 minutes pre-session. Including a briefing of safety guidelines, and practicing to get familiar with all equipments provided.

Trip Length: 1 Day

Price: $120 Adult

Diffuculty: Beginner

Rapid Rush Adventure

This is our most popular intermediate level adventure! This ia an adraneline-pumping adventure for rafting lovers. After a gentle warm-up following the put-in, you will take on incredible, challenging rapids in Taruku Canyon. During this adventure, besides enjoy thrilling rafting experices, you we be able to take a very close look at the breathtaking Taruku Canyon. What more could you ask for?

Before this adventure you will take a 40 minutes pre-session. Including a briefing of safety guidelines, preview of the route, and review of rafting skills.

Trip Length: 1 Day

Price: $150 Adult

Difficulty: Intermediate

Extreme Rapids Challenge

Warning: This is an expert level adventure! This is our all time favorite! If you want to challenge yourself Or if you want to push your limits, this is what you are looking for! Ready for intense drops, narrow chutes and turbulent white water! Our professional licensed team will guide you through the wildest adventure in Hualien. Trust me, this will be the best rafting experince in your life!

Before this adventure you will need to sign a waiver. You are expected to take a 1 hour pre-session. Including an advanced rafting skill coure. After the course you will have to take a test and the minimum score requirement is 70.

Trip Length: 1 Day

Price: $180 Adult

Difficulty: Expert
